Cybersecurity Specialist

Reference: CYB-001

Role Overview

We are seeking a Cybersecurity Specialist to design and operate detection, response and security engineering capabilities. The role includes threat hunting, incident response, security automation and collaboration with cloud engineering teams.

Salary range: £50,000–£75,000 per annum (dependent on experience)

Reports to: Head of Security

Key Responsibilities

  1. Develop and operate detection logic across SIEM and EDR platforms.
  2. Perform threat hunting and incident response, including forensic analysis and containment.
  3. Collaborate with cloud and platform teams to design secure architectures and automation.
  4. Contribute to security playbooks, runbooks and metrics to improve SOC effectiveness.
  5. Perform vulnerability assessments and support remediation prioritization.

Required Qualifications, Skills & Attributes

Essential

  • 3+ years in security engineering, SOC, or incident response roles.
  • Experience with SIEM (e.g., Splunk, Elastic) and EDR tools.
  • Practical knowledge of cloud security (AWS/Azure/GCP) and IaC security patterns.
  • Strong scripting skills (Python, Bash) for automation and enrichment.
  • Good understanding of networking, logging, detection engineering and threat modeling.

Preferable

  • Certifications such as CISSP, CEH, or cloud security certs.
  • Experience with threat intelligence platforms and behavior-based detection.
  • Prior work with security orchestration and automation (SOAR) solutions.

How to Apply

Please include your CV and a short cover letter outlining relevant experience. Shortlisted candidates may be invited to a technical exercise.
