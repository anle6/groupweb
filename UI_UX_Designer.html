UI/UX Designer

Reference: UXD01

Role Overview

We are seeking a UI/UX Designer to create intuitive, accessible interfaces for web and mobile products. The role balances user research, interaction design and front-end implementation guidance.

Salary range: £40,000–£55,000 per annum

Reports to: Head of Product Design

Key Responsibilities

  1. Conduct user research and usability testing; synthesize findings into actionable insights.
  2. Create wireframes, interactive prototypes and high-fidelity visual designs for web and mobile.
  3. Collaborate with product managers and engineers to translate designs into production-ready UI.
  4. Maintain and evolve the design system, components and accessibility standards (WCAG).
  5. Review front-end implementations and provide feedback to ensure fidelity and performance.

Required Qualifications, Skills & Attributes

Essential

  • 3+ years experience in UI/UX or product design roles.
  • Strong portfolio demonstrating UX process, wireframes, prototypes and final UI.
  • Practical skills in HTML, CSS and JavaScript (ES6+) for prototyping and handoff.
  • Proficiency with design & prototyping tools (e.g., Figma, Sketch, Adobe XD).
  • Understanding of responsive design, accessibility (WCAG) and performance best practices.
  • Good communication skills; experience working in cross-functional teams.

Preferable

  • Experience with React or component-based front-end frameworks.
  • Previous work in agile product teams and version-controlled design systems.
  • 2+ years of experience conducting moderated usability tests.

How to Apply

Include your CV and portfolio link when applying. Shortlisted candidates will be asked to complete a brief design task.
